Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

TrueLife ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

To provide our specialized somatic architecture and quote delivery services, we process data in two ways:

  • Locally on Your Device: Your onboarding questionnaire answers, category preferences, target mindset protocols, mood logs, and scheduled notifications are saved strictly on your device's local secure storage (SharedPreferences and SQLite database). This data does not leave your device.
  • Network Cloud Queries: When the App synchronizes daily content, it sends your active category and tag filters to our Firebase Firestore servers to fetch the matching optimized quote payload. This transaction does not contain any personally identifiable information (PII).

2. Third-Party Services

The App integrates trusted third-party SDKs to ensure system performance and support our infrastructure. These services may collect information used to identify your device infrastructure:

  • Google Firebase / Firestore: Used strictly for cloud data architecture, hosting the quote collection, and performing server-side query filtering.
  • Google AdMob: Used to serve advertisements to non-premium operators. AdMob may utilize device identifiers to personalize ad experiences in compliance with standard mobile privacy guardrails.

3. Data Retention and Security

Since the App operates without mandatory user registration accounts, we do not store your personal profile records on external servers. All custom operational configurations remain under your direct control via your physical device storage. Deleting the App will permanently purge all locally cached preferences and SQLite historical logs.

4. Children's Privacy

Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided personal data, we immediately purge it from our local operational scopes.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Updates become effective immediately upon deployment inside the App repository.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or architectural inquiries regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact our support desk through our official GitHub project distribution channel.
